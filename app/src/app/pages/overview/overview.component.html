About Palma-1.0 Dataset

Palma-1.0 is a comprehensive dataset on the world's palms, based on PalmTraits 1.0 and enriched with information from GBIF, iNaturalist, Wikimedia Commons and POWO. It documents the diversity, the origin of palms, and botanic characteristics of palms, an essential botanical family in tropical and subtropical ecosystems.
