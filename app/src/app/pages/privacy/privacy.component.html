1. Introduction

Welcome to Palma 1.0 ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our palm species database website.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices. This policy applies to all users of our platform.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, referring websites

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Research: Understanding palm species information needs for scientific purposes
  • Communication: Responding to user inquiries and providing support

4. Data Sources and Attribution

Our palm species database includes information from:

  • iNaturalist: Community-contributed photographs and observations

  • Scientific Literature: Published research papers and taxonomic databases

All external content is properly attributed according to its original license terms. We respect intellectual property rights and encourage users to do the same.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. We have implemented a cookie consent system that allows you to control which cookies are used:

7.1 Cookie Categories

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality, navigation, and security. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand site usage, performance metrics, and user behavior patterns (with your consent)
  • Marketing Cookies: Used for targeted advertising and social media integration (with your consent)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your search preferences, display settings, and language choices (with your consent)

7.2 Cookie Management

When you first visit our site, you'll see a cookie consent banner allowing you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Accept only essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences for each category

You can modify your cookie preferences at any time by using the "Reset Cookies" option in your browser or contacting us. Your preferences are stored locally and will be remembered for future visits.

Note: Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality and your user experience.
